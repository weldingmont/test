O nama

About us

Weldingmont d.o.o. je kompanija iz Vranja osnovana 2017. godine. Bavimo se izradom i montažom metalnih konstrukcija širom Srbije i Evrope.

Weldingmont Ltd. is a company from Vranje, founded in 2017. We design and install metal structures across Serbia and Europe.

Naše usluge obuhvataju:

Our services include:

  • Metalne konstrukcije i montiranje (hale, objekti, mostovi, šipovi, ograde, stepeništa, nadstrešnice)
  • Metal construction assembly (halls, buildings, bridges, posts, fences, stairs, canopies)
  • Obrada metala: sečenje, savijanje, zavarivanje
  • Metal processing: cutting, bending, welding

Weldingmont d.o.o. je uspešno implementirao sistem menadžmenta kvalitetom ISO 9001:2015 i BS EN ISO 3834. Sertifikaciju je izvršilo renomirano telo Alcumus ISOQAR iz Mančestera.

Weldingmont Ltd. has successfully implemented the ISO 9001:2015 and BS EN ISO 3834 quality management systems, certified by Alcumus ISOQAR of Manchester.
